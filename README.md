# Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

This project analyzes **30,000+ sales transactions** from an e-commerce retail dataset to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and regional performance.

Using **Excel, Pivot Tables, and Power Query**, an interactive dashboard was created to track key sales metrics and business insights.

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts  
- Power Query  
- Basic SQL-based KPI logic  

---

## Dashboard Metrics

The dashboard analyzes:

- Monthly **Orders vs Sales trends**
- **Order status distribution**
- **Sales by platform/channel**
- **Customer demographics (Age & Gender)**
- **Top performing states**
- **Men vs Women sales contribution**

Interactive slicers allow filtering by **month, category, and channel**.

---

## Key Insights

- **Women contribute ~64% of total sales**, indicating a strong female customer base.
- **Amazon and Myntra generate the highest revenue**, making them the most important sales channels.
- **Maharashtra and Karnataka are the top-performing states** in terms of sales.
- **Over 90% of orders are successfully delivered**, showing strong operational performance.
- **Middle-aged customers generate the highest share of purchases.**

---

## Business Decisions Supported

- Focus marketing campaigns on **female customers**.
- Invest more in **high-performing channels like Amazon and Myntra**.
- Expand logistics and promotions in **top-performing states**.
- Monitor return and cancellation rates to improve operational efficiency.
